Tanjay City History

On April 1, 2001, Tanjay City became an official city in the Philippines, but history goes a long way further. Tanjay holds much ancient history for the land being a hub for trade and travel have found artifacts dating back to 200-500 AD. In more recent history the Tanjay mission was founded in 1580 and became a full-fledged parish shortly after in 1589. Tanjay is the oldest Catholic parish on the Oriental coasts.
